Key Features

Advanced privacy-preserving techniques for secure collaborative healthcare AI

🔒

Privacy Preserved

No raw patient data is ever shared between hospitals. Only encrypted model weights are exchanged, ensuring complete data privacy and HIPAA compliance.

🛡️

Differential Privacy

Advanced DP techniques with gradient clipping and noise injection protect individual patient privacy while maintaining model accuracy.

🤝

Federated Learning

Collaborative training across multiple hospitals without centralizing sensitive medical data, enabling better models through shared knowledge.

🔐

Dual Authentication

Enhanced security with separate login and training passwords for authorized model updates, preventing unauthorized access.

📊

Real-time Analytics

Monitor training progress, model accuracy, and system performance through comprehensive real-time dashboards and visualizations.

⚡

Fast Predictions

Make instant CKD predictions using the globally trained model with high accuracy and low latency for clinical decision support.
